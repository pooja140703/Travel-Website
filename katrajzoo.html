RAJIV GANDHI ZOOLOGICAL PARK

Rajiv Gandhi Zoological Park is a must-visit destination for nature and animal lovers visiting Pune, offering a fun and educational experience for all ages.

Information

Rajiv Gandhi Zoological Park, also known as Katraj Zoo, is a popular zoological park located in Pune
The park is home to a wide variety of animals, including tigers, lions, leopards, elephants, zebras, giraffes, deer, monkeys, and many species of birds. The park also has a snake park and a butterfly garden.
The park is open to visitors throughout the year from 9:30 am to 6:00 pm, and the entry fee varies depending on the age and nationality of the visitors. There are also additional fees for some of the attractions within the park.


Timings: 9:30 am–5:30 pm

Speciality

Gandhi Zoological Park, also known as Katraj Snake Park, is among the popular zoos in India. It is in Katraj, only a few kilometres away from Pune. So, it is a favourite among the local people and others who come to explore Pune.
Rajiv Gandhi Zoological Park is divided into three sections: the zoo, a lake, and a snake park. It also has a wildlife research centre, which serves as an animal orphanage, rescue centre and care centre.
You can visit all these parts and watch the animals being cared for in a protective environment. Among the various animals in Rajiv Gandhi Zoological Park & Wildlife Research Centre are leopards, black bucks and elephants. Then, you will find different species of snakes and other reptiles in the snake park.
